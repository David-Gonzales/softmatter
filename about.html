About

Vision

An institution that generates, develops, and transfers technologies and scientific knowledge in synergy with society; through the service of qualified researchers for the integral development of the country.

Mission

An Interdisciplinary, humanistic, competitive, dynamic, and innovative institution that contributes to the holistic development of the country through research, application, and dissemination of scientific knowledge below the guidance of highly qualified researchers.

Strategic axes

Develop and generate innovative scientific research that contributes to the challenges of society

Increase the competitiveness and scientific leadership of associates

Enhance the visibility and dissemination and transferring of academic works and scientific research and results of the institute

Achieve resource efficiency through financing from public and private sources
